Ulleungdo and Dokdo, Treasure Islands in the East Sea
Ulleungdo and Dokdo are treasure islands in the East Sea.
On these unique volcanic islands, there are diverse fish species, plants, and
microorganisms, which make them enormously valuable.
Ulleungdo was selected as one of the world's best secret islands by Lonely
Planet, an internationally renowned travel guide book, for its pristine
nature.
Dokdo was the first Korean territory to fall victim to Imperial Japan in
1905, but it overcome its tragic history, and became the symbol of peace
and freedom.
